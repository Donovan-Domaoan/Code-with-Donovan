About

Hey, I’m Donovan — a software developer with a passion for full-stack development and creating apps that are both functional and fun to use. I work primarily with Python and love diving into challenges that push me to learn and grow. I got into coding because I was looking for something that would truly challenge me — and I’ve been hooked ever since. Whether I’m sketching out ideas or bringing them to life through code, I enjoy every part of the process. When I’m not in dev mode, you’ll probably find me shooting hoops, gaming, learning piano, or geeking out over JDM cars. I’m always building, learning, and leveling up — both on and off the screen. Let’s build something awesome together.
